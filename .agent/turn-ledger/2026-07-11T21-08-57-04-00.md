## Finding

Terrain streamers use `worldSurface.intersectsBounds()`, but grass admits camera-centered chunks without surface membership and balloon movement has no boundary policy. Grass instances are absolute-world transforms inside meshes left at the origin, so manual culling also evaluates every chunk against the same camera-to-origin distance.
